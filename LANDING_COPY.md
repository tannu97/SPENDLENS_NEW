## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial, no freemium trap. We show you the audit for free because it's how Credex finds customers who would benefit from discounted AI credits. If your savings opportunity is large, we'll surface a Credex consultation. If not, you still got a useful audit.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We pull from official vendor pricing pages and cite every number in PRICING_DATA.md. We update when vendors change prices. For API tools, we use your actual reported spend rather than estimating.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. You get the full audit without an account. Email is optional — only asked after you see your results, never before. If you want to save your report or get notified of future optimizations, you can leave your email then.

**Q: Will you sell my data?**
A: No. Your email goes to Credex (the company that built this tool) and nowhere else. We don't run ads and don't sell leads to third parties.

**Q: What's Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — sourced from companies that overforecast. The audits are free; the credits are where Credex makes money. If you have significant AI spend, Credex is worth talking to.
